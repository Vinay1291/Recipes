Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a cake pan.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Gradually add boiling water while mixing.
  5. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Let the cake cool before serving.
