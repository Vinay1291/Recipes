Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook basmati rice until 70% done and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and add mixed vegetables. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric, and salt. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Layer the rice and vegetable mixture in a pot. Cover and cook on low heat for 15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and serve hot with raita.
